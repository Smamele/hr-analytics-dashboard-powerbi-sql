# HR Analytics Dashboard (Power BI + SQL)

## Project Overview

This project analyzes employee attrition, workforce demographics, salary trends, promotions, training hours, and job satisfaction within a South African telecommunications company.

## Tools Used

* Power BI
* SQL
* Excel

## Dashboard Pages

### Executive Overview

* Total Employees
* Attrition Rate
* Average Salary
* Average Years at Company
* Attrition by Department
* Attrition by Province
* Attrition by Gender
* Job Satisfaction Distribution

### Workforce Insight

* Employees Left
* Average Training Hours
* Average Sick Leave Days
* Promotion Rate
* Employees by Department
* Average Salary by Job Role
* Promotion Analysis
* Top Highest Paid Employees

## Key Insights

* Marketing department shows the highest attrition.
* Overtime employees have higher turnover rates.
* Technology and Sales departments employ the largest workforce.
* Promotion opportunities can improve employee retention.
